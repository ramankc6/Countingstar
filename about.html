About

Counting Stars is a calculator for your custom Starbucks beverage, so you can keep track of what's really going into your body. Starbucks doesn't have a way to calculate the calories and macros of custom drinks on their website, only their official drinks are there. We wanted there to be a way for us to keep track of the nutrition of our drinks, so we coded a program that will calculate it for you.
